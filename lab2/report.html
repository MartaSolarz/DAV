Animated Plots: Population Dynamics

Introduction

This report explores the dynamic changes in population across various countries from 1960 to 2022, utilizing data primarily sourced from the World Bank. We focus on three distinct groups of countries: the five most populated, five randomly selected, and five centered around Poland to understand global population trends, growth patterns, and demographic shifts.
